# Power-BI-Sales-Dashboard
📊 Power BI Sales Dashboard

Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard created to analyze sales performance and generate meaningful business insights. The dashboard uses multiple visualizations to help users understand sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and overall business performance.

Dashboard Pages

Page 1 – Sales Overview

- Provides an overview of key sales metrics.
- Displays important KPIs for quick business analysis.

Page 2 – Sales Analysis

- Analyzes sales across different categories and segments.
- Helps identify top-performing areas.

Page 3 – Customer & Product Insights

- Shows customer and product performance.
- Helps understand purchasing patterns.

Page 4 – Regional Performance

- Compares sales across different regions.
- Identifies high-performing and low-performing locations.

Page 5 – Sales Trend by Order Date

- Displays sales trends over time.
- Highlights peak and low sales periods.
- Supports forecasting and business planning.
